> Research_

I work in the HIER Lab on learning-based approaches to legged robot locomotion. My current focus is translating human motion capture data to quadrupedal robots using deep learning-based motion retargeting.

My broader interests are in the algorithmic foundations of robot navigation, agile locomotion, and motion planning. I want to build robots that can safely assist people with tasks that are dangerous, physically demanding, or otherwise out of reach.
